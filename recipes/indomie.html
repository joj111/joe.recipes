Recipe for Indomie

Description

Indomie is a very popular instant noodle in Indonesia.

Indomie is fried noodles (yes, there is no soup), it is best served with an egg.

Ingredients

  • Indomie and seasoning packets
  • Water
  • Egg (if desired)

Steps

  1. Boil water until it is boiling like hell
  2. Put the noodles in and cook for 3 minutes
  3. Drain water
  4. Mix seasoning packets with the noodles
  5. Enjoy!
